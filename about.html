Before Now

I went to Uni in Edinburgh and worked at the Edinburgh Parallel Computing Centre before some of the guys spun off a start-up called Qaudstone. I was a software developer at Quadstone coding traffic microsimulation software before moving into a marketing and business development role.

I lived in Spain for 7 months playing rugby and learning spanish before moving to Sydney, Australia where I worked as an IT Business Analyst. The bulk of my time was spent working for Fox Sports Australia - such a great bunch of people to work with.

I returned to Scotland, with 2 kids in tow, and have been studying for the last few years trying to get my skills back up to speed.
